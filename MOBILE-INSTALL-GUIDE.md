# 📱 How to Install STASH on Your Mobile Device

When you install STASH on your phone, you get:
- ✅ **App icon on home screen** - Just like regular apps
- ✅ **Full-screen experience** - No browser bars
- ✅ **Offline functionality** - Access your stashes without internet
- ✅ **Faster loading** - Instant startup
- ✅ **Native app feel** - Looks and works like downloaded apps
- ✅ **Auto-updates** - Always the latest version
- ✅ **Small size** - Only 1-2MB (vs 50MB+ for native apps)

## 📱 iPhone/iPad Installation (iOS)

### Requirements:
- Must use **Safari browser** (not Chrome or Firefox)
- iOS 11.3 or later

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

#### 1. Open STASH in Safari
- Go to your STASH website URL
- Make sure you're logged in

#### 2. Tap the Share Button
- Look at the **bottom** of Safari
- Tap the **Share icon** 📤 (square with arrow pointing up)

#### 3. Find "Add to Home Screen"
- Scroll down in the share menu
- Look for **"Add to Home Screen"** option 📲
- It may be at the bottom - keep scrolling!

#### 4. Customize and Add
- You'll see the STASH icon and name
- You can edit the name if you want
- Tap **"Add"** in the top-right corner

#### 5. Done! 🎉
- STASH icon now appears on your home screen
- Tap it to open STASH as a full app
- No browser bars, full-screen experience!

### iOS Troubleshooting:

**"I don't see Add to Home Screen"**
- Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome)
- Scroll all the way down in the share menu
- Update to latest iOS version

**"Icon looks weird"**
- This is normal with SVG icons on some iOS versions
- The app will still work perfectly!

**"It's opening in Safari"**
- Make sure you tapped the icon on your HOME SCREEN
- Not the Safari bookmark or recent tabs

## 🤖 Android Installation

### Requirements:
- Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Samsung Internet browser
- Android 5.0 or later

### Method 1: Automatic Prompt (Easiest)

#### 1. Open STASH
- Navigate to STASH in your browser
- Log in to your account

#### 2. Look for the Install Banner
- You'll see a golden banner at the bottom
- Says "Install STASH App"
- Tap **"Install Now"**

#### 3. Confirm Installation
- Android will show an install prompt
- Tap **"Install"** to confirm
- Wait a moment while it installs

#### 4. Done! 🎉
- STASH appears in your app drawer
- Also on your home screen
- Opens as a standalone app!

### Method 2: Browser Menu

#### 1. Open Browser Menu
- Open STASH in Chrome/Edge/Firefox
- Tap the **three dots** (⋮) menu
- Usually in the top-right corner

#### 2. Find Install Option
- Look for one of these options:
  - **"Install app"**
  - **"Add to Home screen"**
  - **"Install STASH"**

#### 3. Confirm Installation
- Tap **"Install"**
- Android will add it to your device

#### 4. Done! 🎉
- Find STASH in your app drawer
- Or on your home screen
- Works just like a native app!

### Android Troubleshooting:

**"I don't see Install option"**
- Make sure you're using Chrome, Edge, or Firefox
- The site must be served over HTTPS
- Try refreshing the page

**"Install button is grayed out"**
- STASH might already be installed
- Check your app drawer or home screen
- If found, uninstall and try again

**"It's not in my app drawer"**
- Check your home screen
- Try searching for "STASH" in app drawer
- Reinstall if needed

## ✅ How to Know It's Installed Correctly

### You'll know STASH is properly installed when:

1. **App Icon Exists**
   - On home screen or app drawer
   - Has the STASH logo

2. **Opens Full-Screen**
   - No browser address bar at top
   - No navigation buttons at bottom
   - Looks like a native app

3. **Stays Logged In**
   - Don't have to log in every time
   - Session persists between launches

4. **Works Offline**
   - Can view cached stashes without internet
   - (Creating new stashes requires internet)

## 🔄 Updating STASH

### Good News: Automatic Updates!

PWAs update automatically. You don't need to:
- ❌ Check for updates manually
- ❌ Download updates from app store
- ❌ Reinstall the app

**How it works:**
- Next time you open STASH
- It checks for updates automatically
- Downloads and applies them in the background
- You always have the latest version!

## 🗑️ Uninstalling STASH

### iOS (iPhone/iPad):
1. Long-press the STASH icon
2. Tap **"Remove App"**
3. Choose **"Delete App"**
4. Confirm deletion

### Android:
1. Long-press the STASH icon
2. Drag to **"Uninstall"** at top
3. Or: Tap **"App info"** → **"Uninstall"**
4. Confirm removal

## 🆘 Common Issues & Solutions

### "I installed it but it opens in browser"
- **iOS**: Make sure you used Safari to install
- **Android**: Reinstall using Chrome
- Try clearing browser cache first

### "It's asking me to log in every time"
- This shouldn't happen with properly installed PWA
- Clear browser cache and reinstall
- Make sure cookies are enabled

### "It doesn't work offline"
- PWAs work offline AFTER first use
- You need to open it online at least once
- Cached data allows offline viewing

### "The icon looks wrong"
- This is a visual issue only
- The app will work perfectly fine
- Future updates may fix icon rendering

### "I can't find it after installing"
- **iOS**: Check all home screen pages
- **Android**: Open app drawer and search "STASH"
- Try searching in device settings → Apps
