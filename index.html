Install STASH as a standalone application on your computer for quick access and full-screen vault experience.

Install STASH as an app on your computer:

  1. Look for the Install icon (⊕ or 📲) in your browser's address bar (usually on the right side).
  2. Or open your browser menu (⋮ or ⋯) and select Install STASH.

To install STASH on your iPhone or iPad:

  1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari.
  2. Scroll down the share menu and tap Add to Home Screen .
  3. Tap Add in the top-right corner to complete installation.

To install STASH on your Android device:

  1. Tap the Menu button (⋮) in your browser's top-right corner.
  2. Select Install app or Add to Home screen.
  3. Confirm by tapping Install in the pop-up prompt.
